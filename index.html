Our Philosophy

"Travel should be organized, not complicated."

We spent years watching travelers juggle multiple bags, fumbling for IDs at airport gates, hunting for charging cables in deep backpacks, or worrying about bags getting wet in sudden rains.

Many global travel brands are overpriced, while cheap alternatives fall apart after a single holiday. We wanted to build something in the middle: high-quality travel gear priced honestly, designed specifically for our commute realities.

Whether you are boarding a domestic flight, catching a weekend train, or taking a daily cab ride—Travel Objects is built to make your transit organized and hassle-free.

Pranav Malhotra Founder, Travel Objects
